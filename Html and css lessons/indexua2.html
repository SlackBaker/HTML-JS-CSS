Recepe of varenyk


Ingredients for dough:

  • Flour 400.g.
  • Water 200.g.
  • Solt 0,5 teaspoon.
  • Oil 3 table spoons.

Ingridients for filling:

  • Potato 800.g.
  • One onion.
  • One table spoon.

Frying for varenyk:

  • One onion.
  • Oil 3 table spoons.

Steps to make dough:

  1. Sift flour
  2. Than add Solt
  3. In flour make a pit
  4. Broke egg and mix it
  5. A little at time add water and mix dough
  6. Ready dough have to stand up

Steps to make filling:

  1. Cut Potato
  2. Than we turn on our meat grinder
  3. Put potato in meat grinder
  4. Chop onions
  5. Put in a bowl
  6. Put some salt and mix

Steps : make varenik

  1. We make small circle from dough
  2. Than make put filling in circle and make moon like from dreeam works but without boy.
  3. Throw them into boiling water
  4. Than boil varenyk for 2 minutes

Steps to make frying for varenyk

  1. Chop onion
  2. Fry them
  3. After that all is ready put them on varenyk
